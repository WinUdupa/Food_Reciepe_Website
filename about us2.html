WHO WE ARE

Yum tum is here to help you cook delicious meals with less stress and more joy.We offer recipes and cooking advice.
For home cooks, by home cooks. Helping create 'kitchen wins' is what we are all about.
YUM TUM was founded in 2023 by team Hack-a-thletes as a student recipe blog to record budget,friendly,tasty and low effort.

Today,Yum TUM has grown into a trusted resource for student cooks with many recipes,guides and meal plans, drawing lot of readers.

RECIPE DEVELOPMENT

We believe in a diet that includes a wide variety of foods: real, butter and cream, exta virgin olive oil, lotrs of fruits and vegetables and protein from meat,fish etc.

There are three things we think about when deciding if a recipe is good enough to go on Simply Recipes:
First, does it work? Recipes need to be easy to follow and provide reliable results, every time.
Second, is it delicious? Does the dish make us smile inside and out? Do we want to eat the whole batch by ourselves?
Third, is it worth the effort? Do we want to make it again (and again and again)? We make sure each of these standards is met so you can have the best recipes at your fingertips.
