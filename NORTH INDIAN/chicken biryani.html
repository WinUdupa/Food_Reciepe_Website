CHICKEN BIRYANI

Chicken biryani is a delicious savory rice dish that is loaded with spicy marinated chicken,onions and flavoured saffron rice.

Ingredients

  • ½kg Chicken
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • ½ tsp Chilli, red
  • 1 Green chili
  • ¼ cup Mint, fine leaves
  • 1 Onion, large
  • 2 tbsp chopped Onions, fried
  • 1¼tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 4 Cardamoms, green
  • 1 inch Cinnamon
  • ¾tbsp Garam masala
  • ¼ tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 pinch Saffron
  • 1¼tsp Salt
  • 1 Star anise
  • 1 Strand mace
  • ¼ tsp Turmeric
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 7 tbsp Yogurt/ curd
  • 3 cups water
  • 6 cloves
  • ¾ tsp shahi jeera

Instructions

  • Marinate the chicken for the Biryani, combine the oil, garlic, ginger, chili peppers, mint, cilantro, garam masala, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl and stir together.
  • Add the chicken pieces and toss together.
  • Allow the chicken to marinate at least for an hour or up to overnight.
  • In a pan add the ghee or butter and onions and saute the onions for15-20 minutes.
  • Transfer the caramelized onions to a bowl and put aside.
  • Prepare rice by washing in a strainer under cold water until the water runs clear.
  • To boil the rice, add the water, salt, cardamom, cumin, and bay leaf to a pot and bring to a boil.
  • And wait for 7 minutes, Drain the rice and reserve 1 cup of the water.
  • In the pan containing caramelized onions ,add the chicken in a single layer, skin-side down.
  • Fry till it becomes golden brown on one side →Flip the chicken over and fry the opposite side until golden brown.
  • Now transfer the chicken back to the bowl you marinated it in.
  • To assemble the Biryani, add the saffron to the rice.
  • Add half of the rice mixture in the bottom of the pan the chicken was browned in.
  • Add single layer chicken on top of the rice.
  • Now add the layer of caramelized onion on top of the chicken.
  • Finish making the Biryani by adding the rest of the rice is a good layer.
  • Add a cup of reserved water from boiling the rice.
  • Cover the pan with a lid, put it on the stove over medium heat, and set it for 20 minutes.
  • Once you can see steam escaping from under the lid, turn down the heat to low for a minute and then off the stove.
  • Without opening the lid, set the timer for one more 10 minutes to steam the Biryani.
  • Mix the chicken Biryani then transfer to a serving platter.
  • Garnish with fresh cilantro
