    • Aloo Tikki

    it is an excotic south indian cuisine special to karnataka. It is rich in its flavour and nutrients


    Ingredients

    • 4 medium potatoes, boiled and peeled
    • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
    • 1 green chili, finely chopped
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 cup bread crumbs
    • Oil for frying

    Instructions

    • In a large bowl, mash the potatoes until smooth.
    • Add the cilantro, green chili, red chili powder, garam masala powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt.
    • Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.
    • Divide the potato mixture into 6 equal portions.
    • Shape each portion into a patty.
    • Coat the patties in bread crumbs
    • Heat the oil in a deep pan over medium heat
    • Fry the patties in batches until golden brown on both sides.
    • Remove the patties from the oil and drain them on paper towels.
    • Serve hot with chutney or raita.

    Tips:

    • For a crispier tikki, use a mixture of bread crumbs and semolina flour.
    • If you want a spicier tikki, add more red chili powder.
    • You can also add other vegetables to the tikki mixture, such as carrots, peas, or corn.
    • Aloo tikki can be stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days.
