PORKANDCHIVE DUMPLINGS

Pork and Chive Dumplings are one of the most traditional dumpling flavors.

Ingredients

For dumpling wrappers

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 2/3 cup Water, hot

    • For dumpling fillings

  • 500 g pork mince
  • 2 bunches garlic chives,finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1⁄2 tablespoon shaoxing wine
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1⁄2 tablespoon oil (optional)

Instructions

For dumpling weappers

  • In a bowl, Add flour and salt into a large mixing bowl and mix
  • While mixing, slowly pour in the hot water
  • Knead the dough with your hands for another 5-8 minutes
  • Place dough in a covered greased bowl, allow to rest for 30 minutes
  • Cut the dough into 16 equal pieces
  • Roll dough into a disc.

For dumpling fillings

  • Place all filling ingredients into a large mixing bowl and mix well
  • To wrap these pork and chive dumplings, place about 1 heaped tbsp of dumpling filling onto the middle of a dumpling wrapper
  • Lightly dab the rims of the dumpling wrapper with water then pleat.
  • Make sure to tightly seal the dumplings so that they don't break apart when cooking
  • Boil the dumplings in lots of water at high flame for 4-5 minutes
  • Finally, Serve as is or with a quick soy sauce
