schezwan fries rice

Schezwan Fried Rice is hot & spicy with bursting flavors of ginger, garlic, soya sauce & red chilli paste.


Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Celery
  • 2 tsp Garlic
  • 2 tsp Ginger
  • 1⁄4 cup Onion
  • 2 tbsp Spring onion greens
  • 2 tbsp Schezwan sauce
  • 1 tsp Soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Tomato ketchup
  • 3 cups Rice, Cooked
  • 1 tbsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp Sesame oil

Instructions

  • Heat the oil in a pan on medium-high heat. Once hot add onion, ginger, garlic and celery.
  • Saute for a minute or two
  • Add all the veggies (carrot, green beans, green peppers, cabbage).
  • Stir fry for 4-5 minutes
  • Now veggies are partially cooked
  • Add all the sauces (schezwan sauce, soy sauce, ketchup, rice vinegar).
  • Mix really well.
  • Add cooked rice and salt.
  • Toss the rice gently so rice grains don’t break and stir fry for 2-3 minutes
  • Lastly, garnish with spring onion greens and serve.
