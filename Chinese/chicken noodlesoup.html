Chicken noodle soup

A classic preparation of chicken noodle soup made with a stewing hen and flavored with thyme and black pepper


Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp Fish Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp Oil
  • 2-3 spring onion, sliced
  • 1 cup Cabbage, shredded
  • 1 Tbsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • 1 medium-sized Chicken Breast , chopped
  • 1⁄4 gallon Water mixed with concentrated Stock
  • 1⁄2 pack Lo Mein Noodles
  • Red Chili Pepper Flakes
  • few drops Sesame Oil (optional)

Instructions

  • First mix together the soy-based sauce ingredients, including brown sugar, fish sauce, soy sauce, and rice vinegar. Keep aside
  • Heat up a pan and add the cooking oil.
  • Then add the sliced spring onions and the cabbage. Stir-fry for 1 minute on high heat.
  • Push all the ingredients aside in the hot pan and add the ginger and garlic, stir-fry for a few seconds
  • Next, add in the chicken pieces and cook them on all sides equally well.
  • Pour in the broth and add soy-based sauce
  • Mix everything well and allow the soup to cook covered for about 7 minutes
  • Minutes before serving time, add noodles to cooking soup.
  • The noodles cook super quickly!
    Serve with chili flakes, few drops of sesame oil and the sliced green onion stalks.
