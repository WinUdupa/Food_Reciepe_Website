SHAVIGE PAYASA

Shavige payasa is a simple Indian dessert made made by cooking ghee roasted vermicelli in milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vermicelli
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 5-6 cashews
  • 8-10 raisins
  • A big pinch of cardamom powder

Instructions

  • Heat the ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add the cashews and raisins and fry until golden brown.
  • Remove from heat and set aside.
  • In the same pan, add the vermicelli and roast for 2-3 minutes, or until lightly browned.
  • Add the milk and sugar and bring to a boil.
  • Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the vermicelli is cooked through and the milk has thickened slightly.
  • Add the cardamom powder and fried cashews and raisins.
  • Mix well and serve hot.
