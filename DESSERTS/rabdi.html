RASGULLA

Rabdi or Rabri is a classic,traditional Indian sweet pf thickened sweetened Indian sweet of traditional sweetened milk filled with layers of clotted cream(malai).

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Pinch of saffron (optional)
  • Nuts and raisins for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  • In a heavy-bottomed pot, bring the milk to a boil over medium heat.
  • Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 30-45 minutes, or until the milk has reduced by half and thickened.
  • Stir in the sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron (if using).
  • Simmer for another 5-10 minutes, or until the rabri has reached the desired consistency.
  • Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  • Serve warm or cold, garnished with nuts and raisins (optional).
